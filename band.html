1. "She Got the Best of Me"
This is the first song that I’ve heard by Luke Combs and I immediately loved it. This breakup song that has been a #1 hit encourgaed me to listen to more of his songs and they do not disappoint!
2. "Fast Car"
This is a cover of Tracy Chapman’s “Fast Car.” Growing up I’ve enjoyed listening to her version, so when Luke Combs came out with a cover it made me love the song even more.
3. "Houston, We Got a Problem"
This song is about how Luke loves where he is, Houston, but it is missing one person in particular. It is significant to me because I love Texas and I was able to hear this song live in the Alamodome, which made it more special.
